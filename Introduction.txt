EMPLOYEE LEAVE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM v1.00
======================================

Welcome to the ELMS system which will be used for Education section organization.

There are 4 types of user which are administrator, HR admin,academic leader and lecturers.Administrator
must login the page in order to allow HR admin, academic leader and lecturer to access the main menu.
This system consists of creation and management of leave policy, leave balancing, FAQs about company, 
list of public and company holidays.User should enter appropriate input for every section in the 
system but not junk input. The yearly employee public and university holidays can by updated,edit and
view.
